My Shiny App

My app contains an interactive graph that shows the probability density function of a normally distributed sequence of numbers.

Data

The app assigns a normal distribution to a sequence of numbers that starts at -30 and ends at 30 with steps of 0.01:

xseq <- seq(-30,30,0.01)
ds <- dnorm(xseq, 0, 1)

How to use App (1)

You can move the two sliders displayed in the sidebar. If you move the sliders, you change the mean and standard deviation used to compute the normal probability density function. A change in the mean moves the density function to the left or right. A change in the standard deviation expands or narrows the bell shape.

How to use App (2)

The plot in the main panel shows how changes in the mean and standard deviation change the probability density function. The plot also shows the standard normal distribution for comparison.
